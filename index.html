📜 This idea is older than money
🌍
Harambee — Kenya's founding philosophy
Jomo Kenyatta built schools and hospitals because every contributor had a claim on what they helped build. That is Hisa.
🤝
Mwethya · Ikundi · Busaa circles
Akamba, Kikuyu and Luo communities ran cooperative systems for centuries: contribute and you have a claim.
📖
Acts 2:44 — the first co-operative
The early church pooled resources. It spread across the Roman Empire because every member had a stake in its survival.
🏭
Mondragon, Spain — 1956 to today
One priest, five workers, no capital. Workers earned shares through labour. Today: 80,000 employee-owners, €12 billion revenue.
